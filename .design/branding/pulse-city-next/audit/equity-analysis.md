# Equity Analysis
> Phase: audit | Brand: pulse-city-next | Generated: 2026-04-15

---

## Reality check

pulse.city is less than a year old, has not publicly launched, and has no measurable audience recognition. **Nothing here is market equity in the traditional sense.** What the brief calls "equity" is really one of three things: internal coherence (the team knows what they mean), founder conviction (Daniel and collaborators want to keep it), or seed signal (something that *could* become equity if preserved through the evolution). This analysis labels each candidate accordingly.

## Asset-by-asset

### Tagline — "the city is playing"
- **Recognition value:** low (nobody knows it yet)
- **Category:** **seed signal — protect** 
- **Why:** it's the one string in the whole product that captures the compass in five words, present-tense, lowercase. It's the line Lia would screenshot. Every other brand artifact should be built to deserve this line. Genuine candidate for future equity.

### Dark base `#0a0e17` + surface ramp `#111827` / `#1e293b`
- **Recognition value:** low
- **Category:** **internal coherence — preserve**
- **Why:** not distinctive vs. peers (half the tools in this space use a near-identical dark blue), but load-bearing for the terminal-warm mood and for the CodeMirror editor's legibility. Preserving it costs nothing and breaks nothing.

### Lime `#a2d729` / Sky `#3aa5ff` / Violet `#6b46ff` trio
- **Recognition value:** low, but the *combination* is mildly distinctive
- **Category:** **seed signal — evolve, not replace**
- **Why:** individually each hex is generic, but the trio on `#0a0e17` is not a combination IPÊ.FM, SomaFM, or Endel use. If the evolution assigns them semantic roles (creator / listener / agent) they become a system with memorability. As unassigned accents they're just "three colors."

### Type pairing: Chakra Petch / DM Sans / JetBrains Mono
- **Recognition value:** low
- **Category:** **mixed — founder conviction on Chakra Petch, genuine signal on JetBrains Mono**
- **Why:** JetBrains Mono is load-bearing — it's the "this is real code" signal that anchors the anti-Suno claim. DM Sans is neutral, fine, replaceable. Chakra Petch is the founder-conviction slot — it's in the brief's "preserve" list but it fights the compass (see coherence-assessment). Call it out as the riskiest preserve.

### Uppercase tracked-widest micro-labels (`SIGN IN`, `SAVE AS`, `OUT`, `PLAYING`)
- **Recognition value:** low, but the *pattern* is highly consistent
- **Category:** **internal coherence — evolve per surface**
- **Why:** this is the most distinctive voice move in the current code. It earns legitimacy on /studio (terminal greeting) and actively harms /radio and /p where the listener needs warmth. The pattern is worth documenting and *rationing*, not applying universally.

### `pulse-dot` and `evolve-glow` animations
- **Recognition value:** none
- **Category:** **placeholder — evolve into a real motion system**
- **Why:** both are opacity pulses on the same curve — they are the *idea* of motion language, not the language itself. Worth preserving the *naming* (pulse, evolve) because it matches the compass, but the actual keyframes are a first draft.

### Metadata line — "A living soundtrack for Ipê Village 2026. Open. Autonomous. Collective."
- **Recognition value:** none (it's a meta tag)
- **Category:** **seed signal — promote**
- **Why:** the three-word rhythm at the end is the best articulation of the personality triad in any artifact. It deserves to leave the `<meta>` tag and become a first-class surface element — on the marketing page, in the embed widget footer, in the docs header.

### Wordmark `PULSE·CITY` (text-only, lime with sky middot)
- **Recognition value:** low
- **Category:** **placeholder — needs a mark**
- **Why:** functional as a temporary wordmark in the studio header but won't survive a favicon, a 16px tile, or a projector far-view. The middot is a cute typographic detail, not a brand asset. Treat it as a starting point for a real mark, not something to preserve as-is.

## What could become equity

If pulse.city preserves these three through the evolution, it has a real shot at earning recognition by Ipê Village 2026:

1. **"the city is playing"** — the line.
2. **Code visibly changing on screen while audio plays** — the anti-Suno move, the thing no competitor can copy without rebuilding their product.
3. **A motion primitive that says "alive and remembering"** — not yet designed, but nameable (pulse / evolve), and the compass demands it.

Everything else in the current system is scaffolding. Treating it as precious would be inertia.
